Salvatore Nunnari

  • Associate Professor of Economics at Bocconi University
  • Research Fellow at BIDSA, CEPR, CESifo, and IGIER
  • Editorial Board Member at PSRM, the Journal of the European Political Science Association

       

I earned a Ph.D. in Social Sciences from the California Institute of Technology in 2012 and have previously served as Assistant Professor at UC Sam Diego and Columbia University. At Bocconi, I am also the Co-Director of BELSS, the Bocconi Experimental Laboratory for the Social Sciences, and the Director of BESS, the B.Sc. in Economic and Social Sciences. My research focuses on political economy, behavioral economics, experimental economics, and microeconomic theory, and it has been published in top general interest journals such as the American Economic Review, Management Science, the American Political Science Review, and the American Journal of Political Science. I recently received a European Research Council grant for a five-year project on the “Behavioral Foundations of Populism and Polarization”.
